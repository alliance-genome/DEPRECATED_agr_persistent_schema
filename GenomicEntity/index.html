Type: genomic entity

an entity that can either be directly located on a genome (gene, transcript, exon, regulatory region) or is encoded in a genome (protein)

URI: https://github.com/alliance-genome/agr_persistent_schema/src/schema/publisher_resource.yaml/GenomicEntity

Children
